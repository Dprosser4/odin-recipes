Classic Lox Bagel

Basic Lox Bagel Facts and Recipe:

Lox bagels are a staple in Jewish delis in New York City.

Quick and Easy, one of the most delicious meals of all time!

Ingredients

  • Bagel of Choice
  • Cream Cheese (savory flavors can be used, like chive)
  • Lox
  • Capers
  • Thin Sliced Onion
  • (Optional) Dill

Steps

  1. Toast the bagel
  2. Spread cream cheese on the bagel
  3. Add desired amount of lox on top of cream cheese
  4. Top with capers, onion, and dill if using.
  5. Enjoy!
