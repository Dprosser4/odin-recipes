Ribeye in Cast Iron

Basic Ribeye Facts and Recipe:

Ribeyes are my favorite cut of beef.

The "cap" of the ribeye is, in my opinion, the best piece of meat on the entire animal.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak (about 1 1/2 to 2 inches thick)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • A high smokepoint oil like avocado oil or ghee.

Steps

  1. Get a cast iron pan as hot as you can.
  2. Add oil.
  3. Pull ribeye from the fridge and season generously with salt and a few cracks of pepper.
  4. Immediately lay meat in the pan and do not move for 3-4 minutes
  5. Flip steak and cook for another 3-4 minutes depending on thickness and desired level of doneness.
  6. Remove steak and plate. Let it rest for a 5-10 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
